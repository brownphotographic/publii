About Rob

By Rob Brown
Published on February 25, 2023

Rob Brown is a professional product manager, and amateur photographer based in Hamilton, MA USA. British by birth, he grew up in the Cotswolds and studied at  Swansea University (Wales), and the University of Nottingham. For the last 15 years he has lived in the US, working as a product manager in robotics and autonomous vehicles.

My pastimes:

  • Dad
  • Amateur Photographer
  • Tinkerer of tractors
  • Fixer of things
  • Open Source software
