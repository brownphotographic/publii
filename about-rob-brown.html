Rob Brown is a professional product manager, and amateur photographer based in Hamilton, MA USA. British by birth, he grew up in the Cotswolds and studied at  Swansea University (Wales), and the University of Nottingham. For the last 17 years he has lived in the US, working as a product manager in robotics and autonomous vehicles.
